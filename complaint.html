How to Complain

  1. Talk to your teacher. Teachers can be surprisingly reasonable. If you do not have a good oppurtunity or do not feel confortable talking to your teacher, skip to step 2
  2. Send your teacher an email. In addition to providing both of you more time to provide a thoughtful response, it documents your complaint in writing in case you need to escalate.
  3. Contact you guidence counselor. You can send an email, or make an appointment at student services before or after school. Your guidence counselor should be able to advise you further
  4. Contact the principal or assistant principal. Initial contact should take place by email. You may recive a reply to your email, or the administrator may request to meet in person
  5. File a parent concern form
  6. Contact the Community Superintendent
You can also contact the Ombudsman, who can direct you individuals in the school system who may be able to help you Find your teacher's email For more information on how to file a formal complaint, visit https://www.hcpss.org/formal-concern/
